Participants

Who can attend UI5con?
UI5con welcomes everyone passionate about UI5 and related projects, from beginners to seasoned experts. Being an open-source initiative, UI5con is not exclusive to individuals within the SAP ecosystem but is open to all enthusiasts eager to explore and innovate with UI5 technology. So, if you are keen on diving deeper into the world of UI5, UI5con is definitely for you!
What is UI5con and what can I expect from the event?
UI5con is the leading community conference for developers with a keen interest in OpenUI5 and its associated ecosystem. The event serves as a dynamic platform that allows developers, experts, and enthusiasts to connect, exchange knowledge, and learn from each other in a collaborative environment. Participants can look forward to meeting the creators of UI5, networking with them, and gaining their insights during "Meet the Expert" sessions. Additionally, participants can interact with developers of various UI5 community projects, sharing knowledge and receiving feedback on their work. This event is an invaluable opportunity for personal and professional growth within the UI5 community.
What is the expected attendance size for UI5con 2025?
UI5con 2025 is anticipated to have approximately 400 participants on-site. The number of participants joining virtually can vary and is difficult to predict.
Do I need to have experience with UI5 to attend UI5con?
While having some experience or basic knowledge of UI5 can enhance your experience at the conference, it is not a requirement. Whether you are an experienced developer or just starting out, your level of expertise does not matter as long as you have a passion for web development, particularly with UI5.
How can I be part of UI5con 2025?
There are several ways to participate in UI5con:

Attend in person: For the on-site experience, you will need to register for a ticket. This grants you access to all the sessions, networking opportunities, and the overall conference atmosphere.

Attend virtually: Cannot make it in person? Join us live online! The main track of UI5con will be streamed on the official UI5 YouTube channel. No registration is required.

Submit a session proposal: Share your UI5 expertise with the community by submitting a session proposal! This is a fantastic way to contribute to the conference and qualify for a free onsite ticket.

Volunteer your time: Help us make UI5con a success! UI5con relies on helping hands to ensure a smooth and successful event. If you are interested in contributing your time and skills, apply to be a volunteer at the conference.

Become a Sponsor: Businesses can support UI5con through sponsorship. Sponsorship packages include complimentary conference tickets and the option to purchase additional tickets for your team.
Who will be speaking at UI5con?
At UI5con, our speakers include a diverse mix of individuals from various sectors who are well-versed in OpenUI5 and associated projects. Our lineup features a dynamic range of presenters—from those taking the stage for the first time to share their initial forays into the technology, to deeply experienced professionals delving into complex subjects. Speakers not only include original framework developers but also feature contributions from users, partners, and the broader community, all bringing unique perspectives and valuable insights based on their specific industry experiences.
Are there any additional events or activities happening alongside UI5con?
Yes, there are several exciting opportunities associated with UI5con. On the day before UI5con, a UI5-focused CodeJam will take place, featuring expert guidance from the OpenUI5 team and SAP advocates. Additionally, UI5con is part of the Code Connect series, which also includes reCAP and HANA Tech Con. These events are scheduled in the same week and at the same location, providing a comprehensive and enriching experience. By exploring reCAP and HANA Tech Con alongside UI5con, participants have the opportunity to enhance their overall conference experience, gaining broader insights and networking possibilities.
How can I stay updated on UI5con news and announcements leading up to the event?
Stay updated on UI5con news and announcements by following this website and subscribing to the UI5con newsletter.

Speakers

When is the Call for Proposals (CfP) for UI5con?
The Call for Proposals for this year's UI5con opens on 23. January 2025, and closes on 10. March 2025, at 23:59 UTC. Make sure to submit your proposal within this period to be considered for the event.
Who can speak at UI5con?
We welcome speakers of all experience levels, from beginners sharing their first project to seasoned experts diving into advanced topics. We strive for a balanced program catering to all skill levels, showcasing content not just from the framework's creators, but also—and importantly—from customers, partners, and especially the wider community. Our goal is a diverse range of perspectives, ensuring a rich and comprehensive program. So, if you have something to share about your UI5 journey, you could be a great fit to be a UI5con speaker!
What makes a good UI5con speaker?
A passion for UI5 and a story to tell! We welcome presentations from all experience levels, covering everything from beginner tips to advanced techniques. Whether you are a customer, partner, community member, or SAP employee, we want to hear your unique perspective on UI5.
What are the benefits of speaking at UI5con?
Speaking at UI5con is your chance for personal growth, allowing you to refine your communication skills, expand your professional network, and establish yourself as a thought leader within the UI5 community.
How do I participate in the Call for Proposals?
To participate in the CfP, simply visit our dedicated Speakers page. There, you will find all the necessary information regarding the CfP, including the submission link and guidelines.
What are the preferred topics or areas of interest for UI5con this year?
UI5con aims to provide a diverse and engaging program for UI5 developers of all skill levels. We value both expert-level content and introductory material that can help individuals kickstart their UI5 journey. While there is no specific theme, we encourage submissions on topics such as sharing experiences from UI5 projects, providing tips for better utilization of UI5 and avoiding common mistakes, delving into specific aspects of UI5 in detail, demonstrating how UI5 can be integrated with other technologies or platforms, discussing real-world case studies and success stories. We also encourage submissions on peripheral topics that might indirectly benefit UI5 developers, including discussions on JavaScript or CSS advancements, design guidelines, user experience optimization, and innovative methods to streamline the development process. However, please note that topics completely unrelated to UI5 are unlikely to be accepted, as our focus is on enriching the UI5 developer community.
How are sessions selected for UI5con?
After the CfP closes, a dedicated program jury will carefully evaluate all submissions and finalize the conference schedule. The selection process is rigorous and impartial, considering factors such as relevance, originality, and speaker expertise. You will find the members of this year's selection committee on the Speaker page (coming soon).
When will I be notified if my session was selected?
All submitters will be notified of the selection results by the first week of April. Please ensure that the email address provided in your submission is accurate and active.
I am not sure if I have a topic. Can I discuss my ideas with someone?
Absolutely! We encourage potential speakers to reach out to us at openui5@sap.com to discuss and refine their ideas. Our team is happy to provide guidance and support throughout the submission process.
Can I submit a proposal even if I have never presented in front of a large audience before?
Yes, UI5con welcomes speakers from all experience levels and backgrounds. If you have valuable insights or experiences to share, we encourage you to submit your proposal. Our team can provide support and resources to help you prepare for your presentation.
Can I submit a proposal also I do not plan to join in person?
Please note that virtual session presentations are not possible.
Can I add co-speakers?
Yes, you can add up to four co-speakers when submitting your proposal, depending on the selected session format. However, please note that all co-speakers must be declared during the CfP. Co-speakers added after the CfP has concluded will not receive an exclusive conference pass.
Are travel and accommodation expenses covered for UI5con speakers?
Unfortunately, we are not able to offer financial assistance for travel or accommodation. However, we will provide you with a complimentary ticket to UI5con and to all other Code Connect events you wish to attend.
What do I need to do regarding UI5con registration as a speaker?
After your session proposal is accepted, we will provide you with a ticket for UI5con and any other Code Connect events you've indicated interest in. You'll receive a confirmation email from support@tito.io with a link to your ticket(s). Please click this link and complete the registration process. This is essential to activate your ticket and ensure your speaker status. You will receive a second confirmation email once registration is complete. If you do not receive these emails, please check your spam folder.

Volunteers

Are there any mandatory meetings for volunteers before the event?
Approximately two weeks before the event, we will hold a live-streamed kickoff meeting for all volunteers. During this meeting, we will provide you with all the necessary information, answer any remaining questions, and share your individual volunteer schedules. Prior to the meeting, we will send you all the relevant details via email to ensure you are well prepared.
Are the volunteers occupied throughout the entire day during the event?
No, we carefully design the volunteer schedule to provide a balance between your volunteer duties and personal time. You will work for a few hours and then have designated free time to enjoy the conference, attend talks, and network with other attendees. We strive to create a schedule that allows you to make the most of your UI5con experience.
What is the application deadline for volunteering at UI5con 2025?
The application deadline for volunteering at UI5con 2025 is 10. April 2025, at 23:59 CEST. Please submit your volunteer application before this date to be considered for the event.
Will my travel and accommodation expenses be covered as a volunteer?
As a volunteer, we will provide you with complimentary drinks and food at the venue during the setup day and the conference day. However, please note that we do not cover travel and accommodation expenses for volunteers.
How do I register as a UI5con volunteer?
Once you have been accepted as a volunteer, we will provide you an entry pass to attend UI5con. You will receive a confirmation email from support@tito.io with a link to your volunteer ticket. Please click this link and complete the registration process. This is essential to activate your ticket and ensure your volunteer status. You will receive a second confirmation email once your registration is complete. If you do not receive these emails, please check your spam folder.

Sponsors

Why should I sponsor UI5con?
Sponsoring UI5con provides you with the unique opportunity to showcase your brand to a targeted audience, demonstrate your commitment to the UI5 community, and enhance your corporate social responsibility efforts.
What are the available sponsorship packages and benefits?
We offer a range of sponsorship packages tailored to meet different marketing needs and budgets: Platinum, Diamond, Gold, and Silver. Each package comes with distinct benefits such as logo placement on the UI5con website and event materials, complimentary tickets, and promotional opportunities. For detailed information on these packages, please consult our Sponsorship Prospectus.
Can sponsors participate in presenting at the event?
Though our standard sponsorship packages do not include presentation slots, we highly encourage sponsors to participate in our Call for Content. We are looking for enriching content that showcases real-world scenarios and thoughtful integration of technology, not sales pitches. More guidelines and submission details are available on our speaker's page.
What are the deadlines for sponsorship registration and material submission?
To facilitate optimal engagement and branding opportunities, we suggest securing sponsorships at least two months prior to UI5con. The final date for submitting your company’s logos, descriptions, and other promotional content is 2. June 2025.
How do I sign up to become a sponsor for UI5con?
To become a sponsor, simply email us at openui5@sap.com. Our sponsorship team will reach out to discuss the diverse options, finalize the sponsorship agreement, and process your payment.
I would like to sponsor but have a different idea of how we can work together.
We value creativity and customization in our sponsorships! If you have an innovative idea on how we can collaborate differently, please share it with us at openui5@sap.com, and we will strive to tailor a sponsorship package that aligns with your vision.

Tickets & Registration

What is the cost to attend UI5con 2025 and do I need a ticket?
Attending UI5con 2025 is completely free of charge. However, if you want to attend in person you will need to order a complimentary ticket to secure your spot onsite. No registration is necessary to attend online.
When will tickets for UI5con 2025 become available and how can I secure my spot?
Tickets for UI5con 2025 will be released on 10. April at 4pm CEST. You can reserve your tickets directly from our event page or via the Tito platform, where you can also secure tickets for the complementary events of Code Connect 2025 series.

After a successful order, you will receive a confirmation email from support@tito.io. If prompted in the confirmation email, complete your registration by assigning each ticket to a unique named attendee and providing all required information. This step is crucial to verify your tickets. You will receive a second confirmation email once registration is complete. If you don't receive these emails, please check your spam folder.
Ensure that you complete the registration within 3 days of receiving the confirmation email to keep your tickets valid.
If I am unable to attend in-person, what options do I have?
Then join us live online! The main track of UI5con 2025 will be streamed on the official UI5 YouTube channel. No registration is required to attend online.
How can I cancel my ticket for UI5con 2025?
You can cancel your UI5con 2025 ticket through your ticket link until 8. June 2025. You can find this link in your confirmation email (sent from support@tito.io). If you cannot find the email, use Tito's Lookup Tool to retrieve it by entering the email address you used to register the ticket.
After 8. June 2025, cancellations must be requested by contacting us at openui5@sap.com. Please include a valid reason for your inability to attend.
Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
Yes, you can transfer your UI5con ticket to another attendee. To do so, find the ticket link in your confirmation email (sent from support@tito.io). If you cannot find it, use Tito's Lookup Tool to retrieve it by entering the email address you used to register the ticket.
Ticket transfers through Tito are only possible until 8. June 2025, at 23:59 CEST.
After this date, please contact openui5@sap.com with the new attendee's full name and email address (cc the new attendee).
Can I share my badge with my colleagues?
No, sharing badges is not allowed. Each participant is required to have their own unique and registered badge. Badge sharing breaches our terms and conditions and hinders our capability to efficiently organize and manage the event.
Why can I only register one ticket for UI5con?
To ensure a fair registration process, we allow only one ticket per person. Since tickets are limited and sell out quickly, we want to give everyone an equal chance to attend. If we detect duplicate bookings, we reserve the right to cancel the additional tickets.
I didn’t receive a confirmation email for my ticket – what should I do?
Please check your spam or junk folder. The confirmation email is sent from support@tito.io. Make sure to keep an eye on that inbox, as all future updates about the event will also come from this address.

Early Bird Ticket

What are Early Bird tickets?
Early Bird tickets are a limited number of tickets available for attendees who need to plan and arrange their travel well in advance, especially those traveling long distances. These tickets allow attendees to secure their spot at the event and make necessary travel arrangements with confidence.
How many Early Bird tickets are available for UI5con?
There are only 30 Early Bird tickets available.
Do I have to pay for an Early Bird ticket immediately or only when the formal registration starts?
Tickets for UI5con 2025 are provided free of charge (please refer to Tickets & Registration above) but come with limited availability. Early Bird tickets are even more restricted to ensure participants with high travel and accommodation arrangements have a chance to get one well in advance of the event.
How do I apply for an Early Bird ticket and what is the latest application date?
To apply for an Early Bird ticket, simply fill out the central Early Bird ticket application form. This form allows you to apply for an Early Bird ticket not only for UI5con but also for any of the other events within the Code Connect 2025 series.
The deadline to apply for an Early Bird ticket is 27. March 2025, at 11:59 pm CEST.
What happens after I submit my Early Bird ticket application?
After submitting your application, you will receive a notification about its status within 2 weeks. If your application is approved, you will be required to book your travel, and provide us with a copy of your travel itinerary to the contact email provided. Your event ticket(s) will be issued after we receive proof of your travel arrangements.
Why do I need to provide travel arrangement proofs after my Early Bird ticket is confirmed?
Providing proof of travel arrangements ensures that those who receive Early Bird tickets have a genuine need and intention to attend UI5con. This process helps prevent misuse of the limited Early Bird ticket availability and ensures that it benefits those who truly need it for planning their travel.
How can I return my Early Bird ticket in case I cannot attend the event?
Please refer to the cancellation process outlined in the Tickets & Registration section above.
Can I apply for Early Bird tickets for multiple people or my team?
No, Early Bird tickets are strictly for individual applicants. For group arrangements, please consider our sponsorship packages, which also include ticket contingents suitable for teams.
What if I do not need to plan my travel far in advance?
If advanced booking is not a requirement for you, regular ticket registration will be available starting 10. April 2025, at 4 pm CEST.

Venue

Where and when is UI5con 2025?
UI5con 2025 will take place on July 8th at SAP University in St. Leon-Rot. The conference will run from 9:00 to 18:00 CEST, followed by an after-event at the same location until 22:00 CEST. Further details on the venue, directions, and travel information can be found on our Participants page.
Is Wi-Fi available at the venue?
Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available onsite. Wi-Fi access details will be provided at check-in and in the event app.
Will there be food and beverages at the venue?
Yes, we will provide food and drinks throughout the event, including breakfast, lunch, snacks, and refreshments including coffee. Vegetarian options will be available for all meals If you have special dietary requirements, please email us (openui5 email) in advance so we can accommodate your needs.
Is there any place to store my bags or luggage?
Yes, there will be a secure room available for bag and luggage storage. Just ask at the registration desk.
Is there a specific dress code?
Not at all! Please wear whatever makes you feel comfortable. We will also be dressed casually.
