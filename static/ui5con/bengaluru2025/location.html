Getting There By Public Transport

  • By Metro:

    The nearest metro station to SAP Labs India is Kundalahalli on the Purple Line of Namma Metro. This elevated station connects the East-West corridor. The station is approximately a 1-minute walk from the SAP Labs campus.
  • By Bus:

    • Route 335-N: Connects Kempegowda Bus Station to Kadugodi Bus Station. ​

    Route 500-F: Operates between Banashankari TTMC and ITPL. ​

    Route V-335E: A Vajra service connecting Kempegowda Bus Station to ITPL.

The bus stop "Kundalahalli" is within walking distance.

Further information on bus schedules can be found on the Official BMTC APP : NAMMA BMTC

Parking Information

Parking is available at the SAP Campus. Follow the signs to the designated parking areas upon arrival.

The closest parking facilities to the venue is MLCP Building. Parking is free.
