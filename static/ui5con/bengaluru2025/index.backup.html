Join us on 8th July 2025!

Where UI5 enthusiasts from across the world unite to share and enhance their knowledge. Initiated in 2016, this premier event offers interactive lectures, workshops, and expert corners. With 400 onsite seats and a livestream of the main stage track, UI5con presents a unique platform for learning, networking, and collaborative growth in the UI5 framework.

On the day before UI5con, there will be a special pre-event CodeJam led by experts from the OpenUI5 team and SAP advocates, offering hands-on experience with advanced OpenUI5 topics; final details to follow.
