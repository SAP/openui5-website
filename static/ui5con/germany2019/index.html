On Friday, June 28th, it was time again: Around 450 enthusiasts from different countries streamed to St. Leon-Rot/Germany to meet peers that share the same passion for UI5 at UI5con@SAP 2019.

The day started with the keynote where Stefan Beck and Peter Müßig walked the audience through latest innovations in UI5 and shared their plans for the future of UI5. Afterwards, the program split up into four parallel tracks and covered a wide variety of topics relating to UI5.

All in all, we had 27 lectures of different length and four 90-minutes hands-on sessions presented by members of the UI5 teams, SAP developers, as well as external UI5 enthusiasts. In addition, participants got a chance to chat with the UI5 experts directly and discuss their specific requirements.

The day ended on a cozy note with snacks and cold craft beer and the opportunity to network with some of the most amazing people in the UI5 community.
